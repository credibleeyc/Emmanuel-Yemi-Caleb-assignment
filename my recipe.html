Brownies

List Of Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Nutella
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. put flour inside a bolw
  2. crack 3 eggs
  3. pour 250g of nutella in the bowl
  4. Mix it
  5. pour the mixture into a baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degree celsius for 15 minutes
  7. Enjoy

Bean Cake

List Of Ingredients

  • Bean
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. soak the beans in a bowl
  2. peel the fore skin when it gets softened
  3. mix the peeled beans with pepper
  4. Grind the mixed beans
  5. Mix the grinded beans with a little water
  6. Fry on the fire with groundnut oil

Spaghetti

List Of Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • groundnut Oil
  • Tomatoes and Pepper
  • Eggs
  • maggi and Salt

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a pot for 15 minutes
  2. pour into the broken spaghetti in a bowl and add salt
  3. allow it to steam for 10 minutes
  4. Fry the eggs with your grated tomatoes and pepper with oil
  5. pour the steamed spaghetti into the fried pepper and egg then mix
  6. Serve and Enjoy

Fried egg

List Of Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • groundnut Oil
  • Tomatoes and Pepper
  • salt
  • maggit

Steps:

  1. slice the tomatoes and pepper
  2. break the eggs into a small bowl
  3. add salt and maggi to the egg
  4. pour the groundnut oil into a frying pan
  5. Fry the tomatoes and pepper for 5 minutes
  6. add the mixed egg to the frying tomatoes and pepper
  7. fry more for few mintes
  8. Enjoy
